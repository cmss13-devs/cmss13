Holder datum for a zlevels data, concerning the overlays and the drawn level itself The individual image trackers have a raw and a normal list raw lists just store the images, while the normal ones are assoc list of [tracked_atom] = image the raw lists are to speed up the Fire() of the subsystem so we don't have to filter through
