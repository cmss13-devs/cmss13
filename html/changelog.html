22 July 2020

TheDonkified updated:

  • Pyro now has a "Fire Shield" that makes them immune to getting burn damage and igniting from fire. Other sources, such as WP smoke, incen bullets, etc will still be able to ignite the pyro spec while the shield is active. The shield lasts 6 seconds with a 15 seconds cooldown.
  • The M240T comes with a buffed underbarrel extinguisher with 500 units of water and the strength to extinguish xvar flames.
  • Specialist abilities can be macro'd. For now, there are only the Specialist-Activation-One and Specialist-Activation-Two verbs. Specialist-Activation-One is for scout cloak and toggling broiler-T fuel type. Specialist-Activation-Two is for activating pyro fire shield. You can still macro the specific verbs for those tools, but these specialist ability macros are there so you do not need to keep adding them when a new macroable ability is added.
  • RNG mechanics related to fire damage and ignition of mobs should be removed. No more flamewalking (unless you're immune). Fire deals the same damage now (no more RNG damage or length of being set on fire). Resisting from fire puts out fires at a consistent amount. Values are subject to change.
  • M240T can be manually reloaded and unloaded as long as you are not wearing the Broiler-T back harness. Once you put on the Broiler-T and fire, it will automatically eject your current magazine. Behavior when wearing the Broiler-T (unable to manually load or unload fuel tanks) is maintained.
  • Mag harness M240T will automatically snap to your Broiler-T instead of your armor slot.
  • All fire extinguishers fit in the fuel tank strap pouches.
  • Xenos cannot be healed while on fire.
  • Moved spec-only verbs to their own unique tabs.
  • Extinguisher can fit in the fueltank backpack (not the one that the pyro spec gets, the one found in the pyro kit/req).
  • Pyro PFC kit comes with an underbarrel extinguisher.
  • Offsets for unbarrel attachments of flamers have been properly set.
  • Pyro and people wearing fire suits will no longer receive extra damage from simply standing on fire (other mobs/humans would not receive any damage, just get ignited).
  • Crusher charge can go over window frames again (pending refactor)
  • Spitter acid spray can go through mobs again
  • Crusher can now go over tables, racks, and the retractable railing to req via charge

  • Added new science machinery sprites based off old sprites left behind by NicBoone, modernised and animated by TobiNerd.
