alardun updated:

  • Equipping the Ghillie suit (and possessing sniper skills) enables the two abilities down below.
  • Ability 1: Prepare Position. After 4 seconds uninterrupted by anything, enter a cloak-like state. Your collision is removed (you still can get shot by non-friendly projectiles), you become near-invisible, and you enable Aimed Shot. Moving breaks the cloak entirely, and shooting make you more visible for 5 seconds. The shooting visibility debuffs stack and only resets once you stop firing for 5 seconds.
  • Ability 2: Aimed Shot. Can only be used while in full stealth. Display a lock-on on the target and channels for 1.25 seconds, then automatically fire at the target if the bullet has a chance to connect (No turf/blocking structure in the way/ Line of sight is still there). The shot is homing, though somewhat dodgeable if you can get an obstacle in time. If the shot connects with the intended recipient, the projectile does a critical hit, described below.
  • Added a critical hit effect to Sniper Marksman Bullet. It deals 3x the normal amount of damage (70 -> 210).
  • Added a critical hit effect to Sniper Incendiary Bullet. It maxes out the fire stack if they weren't already (they typically are), and blinds for ~2 seconds on Big Xenomorph (including fire-proofed one), ~5 for anything else. (Blind is the eye-blurry effect you've seen on marines).
  • Added a critical hit effect to Sniper Flak Bullet. The backlash is removed, but the base damage in doubled (Roughly a wash in term of total damage). In exchange, Big xeno are slowed for 4 seconds, and anyone else is slowed for 7 seconds.
  • Pounces will still stun knocked down/collision-less marine if they land on their specific tile.
  • Specialist Kits (Defcon 2) gives the specific skill for the chosen specialist, rather than all specialists.
  • You can now toggle middle/shift clicking for abilities mid-round in your preferences.
