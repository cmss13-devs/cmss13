Commander,

Atlan Station is a UPP-controlled mining platform over the gas giant KG-256.

Originally a Cosmos Exploration Corps settlement, the UPP military has been running more and more of the station in recent years due to it's strategic importance.

The station has been overburdened, unable to meet the Helium-3 qoutas that have been increasing due to increased UPP ship prescense in the region.

Your mission is to respond to the station's recent distress signal, determine the status of UPP civilians and forces and secure any UA interests.
PRIMARY OBJECTIVES
  • - Establish a Forward Operating Base at a location with access to the processing complex.
  • - Locate and evacuate any UA nationals and civilian contractors.

SECONDARY OBJECTIVES
  • - Survey key infrastructure for structural instability or sabotage.
  • - Gather intelligence on UPP troop deployments and PAP patrol routines.
  • - Maintain neutral posture unless directly engaged by UPP forces.

Maintain professional rules of engagement.
