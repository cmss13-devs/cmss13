Commander,

Sorokyne Strata is a jointly operated site, split between a Weyland-Yutani research installation and a mining colony administered by Union of Progressive Peoples contractors.

Both have gone dark.

Your orders are to secure both installations and assess threat levels.
PRIMARY OBJECTIVES
  • - Establish a Forward Operating Base at a location which offers access to both the UPP mining complex and research dormitories.
  • - Locate and recover civilians or staff from both facilities. Focus on scientific personnel and miners.
  • - Secure biological samples, dig logs, and lab drives from the geological research wing.

SECONDARY OBJECTIVES
  • - Ensure reactor safety at both outposts to avoid chain-collapse.
  • - Report presence of UPP military advisors or hostile activity.
  • - Sweep caves and sublevels for signs of Xenomorph infestation. Neutralize as necessary.

Engage with caution. Political fallout is possible.
