[b]SUBJECT[/b]: Operational Objectives - Sorokyne Strata AO

Commander,  

Sorokyne Strata is a jointly operated site, split between a Weyland-Yutani research installation and a mining colony administered by Union of Progressive Peoples contractors. Both have gone dark. Your orders are to secure both installations and assess threat levels.

[b]PRIMARY OBJECTIVES[/b][list][*]Establish a Forward Operating Base at a location which offers access to both the UPP mining complex and research dormitories.  
[*]Locate and recover civilians or staff from both facilities—focus on scientific personnel and miners.  
[*]Secure biological samples, dig logs, and lab drives from the geological research wing.[/list]
[b]SECONDARY OBJECTIVES[/b][list][*]Ensure reactor safety at both outposts to avoid chain-collapse.  
[*]Report presence of UPP military advisors or hostile activity.  
[*]Sweep caves and sublevels for signs of Xenomorph infestation—neutralize as necessary.[/list] Engage with caution. Political fallout is possible. Maintain professional neutrality in all civilian encounters.

For the Corps,  
Second Lieutenant Lucas Harlan
Strategic Liaison Officer
